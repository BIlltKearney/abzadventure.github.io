Ann Kearney's BIO

Ann Kearney is a Connecticut native and simple living advocate. She is a homeschooling mother of three, plant-based cook, outdoot enthusiast, RN, former IBCLE (International Board Certified Lactation Consultant), retired LLL (La Leche League) Leader, traveler, hiker, and lifelong learner.

Ann spent two years living in an RV while traveling the country with her husband and three kids. In preparation for this journey,they sold or gave away around 90% of what they owned, including two cars, and downsized from a 2400 sq.ft. home with a two car garage, yard and shed, to a 192 sq.ft. RV, while only storing a few bins at friend's homes.

She and her husband have remodeled three houses and are currently living in, and remodeling, a foreclosure in the woods of Connecticut with their three children.

Ann's passion is to share the rewards of simple living while continuing her own learning journey alongside her family.
